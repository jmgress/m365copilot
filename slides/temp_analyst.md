# **The Analyst Agent: Your Data Expert**

## **What Is It?**
An AI agent that analyzes data and creates visualizations

## **Key Capabilities:**
- Upload files (Excel, CSV, JSON) and get instant insights
- Ask questions about your data in natural language
- Automatically generates charts and graphs
- Performs statistical analysis and trend detection
- Creates summary reports from complex datasets

## **Example Use Cases:**
"Analyze this sales data and show me trends by region"  
"Find outliers in this customer feedback dataset"  
"Create a visualization comparing Q3 vs Q4 performance"
